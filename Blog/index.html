Building Skills, Building Futures: Strengthening TVL Education

TVL (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood) strands equip students with skills needed in the real work setup. However, many schools do not have enough workspace and materials, which limits students' capabilities to study and practice.

Why It Matters

TVL is not just a subject; it is preparation for life. But barriers are made due to the lack of resources:

  • Limited practice: students have to wait for their turn to use one tool or computer.
  • Outdated equipment and tools in the institutions.
  • Overcrowded Spaces – Small workshops limit learning and safety.

This issue also touches upon the future specialists who will work for the benefit of our country. If TVL is strengthened, the country will produce quality and skilled graduates who can compete for job opportunities both locally and outside the country.

Challenges: What TVL Students Face Every Day

Limited Tools and Equipment

Students have to take turns when practicing on one or two machines.

Insufficient Workspace

Small, cluttered rooms give no space to move, practice, or concentrate.

Lack of Materials

Students cannot finish their project works because the materials ran out or are unavailable.

Unequal Practice Time

Students who spend less time practicing graduate unprepared for real jobs.

Our Goals: Improve Learning Conditions for TVL Students

Short-term Goals

  • Raise awareness about the lack of workspace and materials.
  • Encourage schools and communities to support TVL programs.

Long-term Goals

  • Push for government and industry support in providing tools and facilities.
  • Create an environment where every TVL student can practice confidently.

Our Vision

With the right support, TVL can be more than just sitting in a classroom. It can be a place where we really learn, practice our skills, and prepare for the future. We imagine having enough tools, proper spaces, and the chance to gain confidence in what we do.

TVL is not just a strand but the path to opportunity. Every student deserves to walk that path ready and assured that our education and future matter. Together, let's build better TVL schools today for a brighter future tomorrow.
